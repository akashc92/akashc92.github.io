I am a creative coder and content writer, which I pursue as a side hobby. I work alongside Siddhant Sharma, who is a prospective film director and currently studying at DePaul's University in Chicago. The following are some experimental videos we made when we got time to work off our academic commitments. Some of these videos are made in India and some in Chicago. We juggle our work hence and forth and I was mainly responsible for writing the scripts of the videos, meanwhile also figuring out music themes and foley sound work for the projects. These videos serve as a motivation for me to learn film scoring and thereafter produce high quality videos after upgrading my skills with some professional assistance. Nevertheless, I am full of intuition driven stories that I strongly feel about and will continue on my endeavor to constantly create new media which embed in them a subtle understanding of human psychology, emotions and language.
